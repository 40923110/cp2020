PCH12-Carrier Sense Multiple Access-Collision Detect(載波偵聽多路訪問-衝突檢測)p. 92

The nature of half-duplex transmission brought about the need for CSMA/CD, which works by allowing all devices to transmit data whenever they wish. The carrier sense portion means that the device “listens” to the network first to find a “break” in the conversation. If the device doesn’t hear other devices transmitting, then it begins its transmission. Multiple access refers to every device having equal access to the network at all times. This is unlike the token ring network in which each device had to wait for the signal (token) before transmitting. Finally, we have collision detection as part of the CSMA/CD control method. If, by chance, two devices both were ready to transmit, they “listened” to the network for a “lull” in the conversation, and both started transmitting at the same time, the devices are able to detect that they both transmitted at the same time and caused a collision.

半雙工傳輸的性質帶來了對CSMA / CD的需求，它允許所有設備在需要時傳輸數據。運營商感知部分意味著設備首先“監聽”網絡以在會話中找到“中斷”。如果該設備聽不到其他設備的傳輸，則開始傳輸。多路訪問是指每台設備始終具有對網絡的平等訪問權限。這與令牌環網絡不同，在令牌環網絡中，每個設備都必須在發送之前等待信號（令牌）。最後，我們將碰撞檢測作為CSMA / CD控制方法的一部分。如果偶然地兩個設備都準備好發送，則它們在通話中“偵聽”網絡中的“失敗”，並且兩個設備都同時開始發送，則這些設備能夠檢測到它們都在發送時發送。同時又造成了碰撞。
The image below depicts both the CSMA/CD and CSMA/CA simplified algorithm. The left branch is CSMA/CD used by half-duplex Ethernet and the right branch is CSMA/CA used by 802.11 wireless LAN, which is also half-duplex.
下圖描述了CSMA / CD和CSMA / CA簡化算法。左分支是半雙工以太網使用的CSMA / CD，右分支是802.11無線LAN（也是半雙工）使用的CSMA / CA。

在下面顯示的示例中，四個設備以物理總線拓撲連接。每個設備對網絡都有平等的訪問權限。在這裡，所有設備都在監聽，試圖確定是否有其他設備在開始傳輸之前進行通信。

在下一張圖片中，我們看到了兩個同時傳輸的設備的示例。這種同時傳輸導致數據衝突，導致傳輸中斷。

When a collision is detected, both devices choose a backoff time at random, and then attempt to retransmit again. In the diagram below, the computers choose to wait 200 milliseconds and 300 milliseconds, respectively. When that time is up, they will attempt to sense if another machine is already transmitting. If there is nothing detected, the first machine will transmit its message. Once completed, the second machine will then transmit its message, after its 300 millisecond wait time.
當檢測到衝突時，兩個設備都會隨機選擇一個退避時間，然後嘗試重新傳輸。在下圖中，計算機分別選擇等待200毫秒和300毫秒。時間到時，他們將嘗試檢測另一台機器是否已經在傳輸。如果未檢測到任何內容，則第一台機器將發送其消息。完成後，第二台機器將在等待300毫秒後發送其消息。

Recovering from a collision with random backup timers. Image used under CC-BY-SA license from Nattydusadee.
使用隨機備份計時器從衝突中恢復。圖像由Nattydusadee根據CC-BY-SA許可使用。


Collision Domains 碰撞域

The concept of collision domains is very important in Ethernet standards, because collision domains determine how fast the network will actually operate. As shown in the previous diagram, collisions cause the devices to back off, wait, and then retransmit. If this occurs too often, valuable time is wasted and frequent collisions bring network speeds down - somewhat like a traffic jam on an interstate. All devices on a shared Ethernet segment are members of a single collision domain. If you are running Thicknet or Thinnet (coaxial bus networks), then all devices on a single cable are in the same collision domain. If you are using a star topology with unshielded twisted pair, then all devices connected to a singular hub (since hubs repeat the transmissions on every port) are part of a single collision domain operating at half-duplex.
衝突域的概念在以太網標準中非常重要，因為衝突域決定了網絡實際運行的速度。如上圖所示，衝突導致設備退避，等待，然後重新傳輸。如果這種情況經常發生，則會浪費寶貴的時間，頻繁的衝突會降低網絡速度-就像州際公路上的交通擁堵一樣。共享以太網網段上的所有設備都是單個衝突域的成員。如果運行的是Thinnet或Thinnet（同軸總線網絡），則單根電纜上的所有設備都在同一沖突域中。如果您使用的是帶有非屏蔽雙絞線的星形拓撲，則所有連接到單個集線器的設備（因為集線器會在每個端口上重複傳輸）都是在半雙工模式下運行的單個衝突域的一部分。
To prevent collisions in the network, the collision domains must be kept small. One option is to use bridge connecting hubs to break up the collision domains. However, an even more effective method of doing this is to use switches. Remember, hubs create collision domains while switches break up collision domains but also create broadcast domains.
為了防止網絡中發生衝突，必須將衝突域保持較小。一種選擇是使用橋接連接集線器來拆分衝突域。但是，一種更有效的方法是使用開關。請記住，集線器創建衝突域，而交換機分解衝突域，但也創建廣播域。

One collision domain. Image used under CC-BY license from Jason Dion.
一個衝突域。圖片由Jason Dion根據CC-BY許可使用。
Ethernet switches are preferred over hubs due to their ability to solve the collision domain issue. For the most part, switches have replace hubs; it is rare to see hubs and bridges in current networks because of the superior operation of switches and the overall price reduction.
以太網交換機比集線器更受青睞，因為它們具有解決衝突域問題的能力。在大多數情況下，交換機都可以更換集線器。由於交換機的出色操作和整體價格降低，很少會在當前網絡中看到集線器和橋接器。
Each switch port is its own collision domain and supports full duplex (simultaneous TX and RX) when operating at 100Mbs or faster and using CAT5 cabling or better. This prevents any chance of collision, eliminates the need for CSMA/CD, and increases the speed of the network dramatically compared to hubs.
每個交換機端口都是其自己的衝突域，並且在以100Mbs或更快的速度運行並且使用CAT5電纜或更高規格的電纜時，支持全雙工（同時進行TX和RX）。與集線器相比，這可防止發生任何碰撞的機會，消除了對CSMA / CD的需求，並顯著提高了網絡速度。
